
## Chorpleth

Choropleth maps provide an easy way to visualize how a variable varies across a geographic area or show the level of variability within a region.

This visualisation shows the spatial data visually as a choropleth chart. Choropleth charts provide an easy way to visualize how a variable varies across a geographic area and show the level of variability within a region. The color or shading indicates the value being represented.
                   A grid of maps facets each of the dataset's relevant variables. This chart is only available for spatial datasets.
